# 🏆 DayChallenge

**Build habits that stick.** Track your daily challenges, maintain streaks, and achieve your goals.

---

## ✨ What You Can Do

✅ **Create Challenges** — Set goals for 7, 14, 21, 30, 66, 100 days, or forever  
✅ **Track Progress** — Watch your streak grow 🔥  
✅ **Streak Freeze** — Missed a day? Use one freeze to keep your streak alive  
✅ **Continue Forever** — Complete a challenge? Start a new round  
✅ **See Statistics** — Beautiful heatmaps and progress charts  
✅ **100+ Emojis** — Pick the perfect icon for each challenge  

---

## 🚀 How to Get Started

1. **Download the app** from Google Play Store
2. **Create your first challenge** — Give it a name and choose how many days
3. **Pick an emoji** — Choose an icon that represents your goal
4. **Complete it daily** — Tap "Done" every time you finish
5. **Watch your streak grow** — Stay consistent and reach your goal! 🎉

---

## 💡 Tips for Success

- **Start small** — Try 7-day challenges first to build confidence
- **Keep it simple** — One sentence descriptions work best
- **Use streaks wisely** — Your streak freeze is precious; use it when you really need it
- **Check your calendar** — See when you're most active

---

## ❓ FAQ

**Can I do multiple challenges?**  
Yes! Create as many as you want.

**What if I miss a day?**  
Your streak breaks, but you can use your **Streak Freeze** once per challenge to save it.

**Can I change my mind?**  
Yes — delete or pause any challenge anytime.

**What happens when I complete a challenge?**  
You can start a new round with the same duration. Your total days keep counting!

**Will you remind me?**  
Yes! The app sends daily reminders (coming soon in the next update).
